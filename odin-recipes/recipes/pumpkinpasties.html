Pumpkin Pasties

How to make homemade Pumpkin Pasties

One of the many delicious treats Harry tries on the Hogwarts Express, pumpkin pasties are tasty pumpkin filled pastries that invoke a feeling of coziness and fall.

This recipe is well worth the work and a great gift for your Harry potter loving friends around the holidays!

Ingredients

Pastry dough
  • 1 1/2 cups flour
  • 1 TBS sugar
  • a pinch of salt
  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 2-3 TBS very cold water
Pumpkin Filling
  • 1/2 cup pumpkin puree
  • 1/4 TS cinnamon
  • a pinch of allspice
  • a pinch of cardamon
  • a pinch of nutmeg
  • 1 TBS sugar
Egg Wash
  • 1 egg yolk
  • 2 TBS milk

Steps

Pastry dough
  1. cube and freeze the butter.
  2. mix flour, sugar and salt in a stand mixer.
  3. pulse or mix in the butter cubes until the mixture resembles bread crumbs.
  4. sprinkle the cold water and mix until the dough comes together.
  5. refrigerate dough for at least 2 hrs.
Pumpkin filling
  1. mix the pumpkin puree with the spices and set aside.

Assemble the pumpkin pasties

  1. prheat oven to 400 fht.
  2. roll out the dough and cut into circles.
  3. spoon 1/2 TS filling onto one side the pastry circle and fold the other side over.
  4. use a fork to crimp the edges of the pastie.
  5. cut vents into the pasties.
  6. place the pasties on a baking tray and brush with the egg wash.
  7. bake for 15 to 20 minutes until golden brown
  8. let cool on a wire rack completely
